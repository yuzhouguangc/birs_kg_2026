This workshop will take place from June 15–19, 2026 at the Banff International Research Station (BIRS), Oaxaca, Mexico. The program features invited presentations from leading researchers in mathematics, statistics, artificial intelligence, and knowledge graph reasoning, together with collaborative working-group discussions, a cultural excursion to Monte Albán, and dedicated sessions for open scientific exchange. Events marked 🏛️ denote in-person presentations, while 🌐 indicate remote presentations. All times are approximate and listed in the local Oaxaca timezone (UTC−6).
