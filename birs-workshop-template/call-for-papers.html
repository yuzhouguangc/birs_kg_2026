Call for Papers

We invite submissions on topics including, but not limited to:

  • Foundation models for scientific discovery
  • AI agents for scientific reasoning
  • Multimodal learning for scientific data
  • Knowledge graphs and scientific representation learning
  • Trustworthy and interpretable AI for science

Submission format: Papers should be 4--8 pages, excluding references and appendices.
